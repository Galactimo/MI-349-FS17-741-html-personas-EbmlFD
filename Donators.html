Who is Bill?

Goals

Bill's Motivations come from a place of altruism. Although in a more deeper context, Bill needs to be seen as a good person. Also, Bill wants to have children and believes that we need to be able to leave our planet to survive. That being said he donates to make a future for his progeny

Attitude

Bill sees Space Scout as a means to an end he desires. Maybe the only means to the end he wants, as NASA and other companies are not pushing manned space travel these days.

Behavior

Bill is meticulous in researching what he invests in. He will only invest also if there is proof that Space Scout is pioneering in Space fairing technology. He would most likely find out about the company through either the internet or word of mouth from other prominent investors.
