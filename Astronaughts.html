Who is Gary?

  • Age: 26
  • Location: Cape Canaveral, Florida

  • Personality: Gary has not much for him on the earth, he is single and has only really ever loved the stars. He already has been in space but never really has gotten the oppurtunity to explore the stars.

Goals

Gary has hardly any ties to family left on earth. He wants fame and recognition from being one of the first astronaughts sent on a manned exploration mission. He needs to always be exploring something new, and currently there is not much left on our earth left unexplored.

Attitude

Gary is dissatisfied with just being in a space station orbiting the earth. Gary knows that NASA isn't trying any manned deep space exploration missions any time soon. That makes him ready to jump ship if an oppurtunity like what Space Scount means to provide pops up. That makes Space Scout an oppurtunity for him to fuffil his lives dream.

Behavior

Gary is fearless, he knows that outer space is a dangerous place, but that wont stop him from his dream. He actively searches for any oppurtunities to explore the stars and hasn't quite found what he is looking for yet. He is quite patient and will make certain to find the best company doing exploration, as his life will be in their hands.
